About Me

Hello!

I am Pedro, from Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Digital designer currently working with Front-end Development and studying Computer Science. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Design and a Graduate Diploma in Digital Game Development.

Studying design helped me to observe and understand how people interact with objects, how human relations are being influenced by technology, and how social behaviors are changing during the years. During the bachelor’s degree, I could stay involved with some projects related to technology in education and its social impacts. The user experience study and the improvement of the learning process were my focuses. It guided me to study educational games and gamification techniques.

I believe that entertainment is an effective education tool that helps the learning process through playfulness, interactivity and sharing information. Design study gave me the ability to think critically and plan all the steps of the project. However, from my point of view, design cannot work without technology. So, I decided to focus on improving my programming skills, in order to build my own projects and achieve better job opportunities.

Now, I am learning about React and React Native development, and how to integrate all these things with a Node.js back-end system. You can see some of these projects on my dev portfolio.
